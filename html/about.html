ABOUT

OpenFarmsubsidies is a search engine crawling through the subsidy databases of the EU member states.

It is a free and open service intended to be used by journalists, scientists and an interested general public.

Some Goodies...

  • Unified search interface for payments in various EU member states
  • Blazing fast! :-)
  • Full-text search, various search filters
  • Converted non-€ currencies to €
  • Translation/search for non-latin languages
  • Aaaaaaand a responsive interface, so search farm subsidies on the go! :-)
