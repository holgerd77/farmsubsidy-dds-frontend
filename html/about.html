ABOUT

OpenFarmsubsidies is a search engine crawling through the subsidy databases of the EU member states.

It is a free and open service for journalists, scientists and the interested general public.

  Unified Search

Subsidy payments can be searched by name or town across the various EU member states.

  Filters

Static filters for country, year and amount and dynamic filters for town and sub payment type (EAGF, EAFRD,...) help to drill down to the subsidies you are looking for.

  Euro Currency Conversion

All payments from non-euro countries are converted for easier subsidy comparison and filtering.

  Translation

Non-latin language names are translated and are also available for search.

  Investigative Tools

Simple but powerful tools allow for further investigation with services like Google, Wikipedia, OpenStreetMap or OpenCorporates.
